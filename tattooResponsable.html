Responsible tattooing

Communication and awareness campaign

2024

My diploma project took the form of a prevention campaign aimed at young people (16-24 years old) whose goal was to promote the practice of professional tattoo artists in order to encourage the target to behave responsibly in their tattooing practice (choosing your tattoo artist well means choosing your tattoo well). The goal was to transform the body into a museum and for the target to become its curator. Thus I highlight the statue of a work of art that the tattoo has and I show to the target the responsibility they have when they decide to get a tattoo. This analogy is also a way to show different contexts where we choose to get tattooed and the variety of styles.
