About

I'm a graphic design student interested in typography, letterforms, and what they try to tell us beyond words. In their intentions and the feelings they express. What their lines tell us about their past, and I like to imagine their future form.

I began my academic career in graphic design in High school with a Applied Arts specialized teaching, then a Bachelor degree in Graphic Design. In September 2025, I will begin a master degree in Editorial Design.
