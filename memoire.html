Essay

Research, Edition

2023

Essay conducted during the final year of my bachelor degree. I was interested in the world of tattooing, and more specifically how graphic design could play a role in its good practice among young people. Encourage them to adopt responsible behavior, limit the number of regretted tattoos. But also to help create a professional and responsible image of tattoo artists. During my study, I noted the poor reception of prevention campaigns among the target audience. I explored what levers could be used to make the target audience more attentive.
