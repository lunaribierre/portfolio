Type Exquisite Corpse

Workshop, Sign Painting

2023

Banners created during an introductory letter painting workshop led by Hélène Marian. The task was to constrain the letters of a word to fit it into a random format. The lettering was based on typography gleaned from gravestones in a cemetery. This exercise allowed me to reflect on how to play with the construction of the letter. To understand its characteristics so as not to distort it. The exercise is also interesting by the scale of the letter, as I'm more accustomed to working with it in small formats. Here, it's like meeting it personally. Being able to truly see each of its particularities and thus facilitate this modification work without disfiguring it.
