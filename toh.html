Tom of Holland

Visual Identity

2022

Tom of Holland is a visible mending designer. He needs to make his business and values known and establish his artistry image. To do this, he needed a marker of identity. He is a creator who asserts, displays, enhances, magnifies, and sublimates... the traces of time. I used the evocation of a life cycle, alternating between degradation and regeneration, to convey the notion of "sublimating the traces of time." The use of Baskerville evokes a traditional craftsmanship found in Tom of Holland's creations.
