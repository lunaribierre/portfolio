Stocker

Type design, Edition

2023

Stocker is a project of font reconstruction from a limited number of characters. Stocker is similar to the typefaces used on this genre film posters (imperial, narrow, and expressive). I wanted to pay homage to them by creating a specimen based on the Dracula films, also allowing for the highlighting of its angular serifs on some of the letters.
