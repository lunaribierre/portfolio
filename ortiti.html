Ortiti

Branding, Visual identity, Type design

2024

Branding proposal for a clothing brand made exclusively from nettle fiber. After analyzing the promotional context for a little-known, innovative, and eco-friendly product, I chose to create a brand dedicated to future generations: children. It's aimed at parents who want the best for them. Ortiti is an ode to children who are unapologetic themselves. A garment made to accompany them on all their adventures. I brought together the child's still-wild spirit and the plant's prickly nature, through a reference to the punk movement, which advocates a rejection of authority and decency.
