Crisis leaflet

Edition, Software Experimentation

2025

For this project, I was asked to lay out a text taken from the crisis leaflets published by Gallimard. The challenge was to experiment with the tools offered by InDesign. The basis for this experiment is a text by Jacques Drillon, "Je veux." The text explores all the author's desires during the COVID crisis. In my experiments, I sought to represent the dissonance that arises between his desires and reality. The feeling of frustration that this situation creates. With text that fights justification, paragraphs that step on each other's toes...
